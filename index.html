Who we are

Our players come from various points of their soccer journey.

We're also proud to be a welcoming space for people who haven't always felt comfortable in mainstream sports, including women, trans, and nonbinary players. What everyone has in common is they're here for a friendly space.

How to play with us

Apply to join our Meetup group. Hit the Join us button on our Meetup page and fill out your profile. We only approve people who give us the basics: a clear photo of just you, a real intro, and your real first name.

Once you're approved, you'll be able to RSVP for games when spots open each week.
