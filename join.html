How to get in, and what you agree to as a member.

Become a member

We organize through Meetup. To apply, fill out your profile with:

  • —A clear photo of just you
  • —A real intro about yourself
  • —Your real first name
  • —Your pronouns

Once approved, you'll be able to RSVP for games as spots open each week.

We have separate RSVPs for WTNB (Women, Trans, Nonbinary) players and men players. Games are posted weekly and spots fill fast. RSVP early.

Find us on Meetup →

Member expectations

Attendance

  • You must be on time for the start of the games. After our intro speech and then once teams are set, latecomers can't join. You will be marked as a no-show.
  • Stay for the full two hours, unless you're injured.
  • Three no-shows in a season means a ban for the rest of that season.

What to bring & wear

  • No cleats, no exceptions. Turf shoes or sneakers only.
  • Bring a ball if you have one. We always need more.
  • Shin guards are optional and not needed.
  • Bring water. Games are 2 hours and we play rain or shine.

Respect

  • We are a welcoming space. Use people's correct pronouns and treat everyone with kindness.
  • We have zero tolerance for racism, homophobia, transphobia, or any other form of discrimination. Anyone who violates this will be removed from the group.
  • You'll be playing alongside people of all backgrounds. Different skill levels, different countries, different ways of knowing the game. Be kind, patient, and supportive of everyone on the field.

Consequences

  • If you need to be spoken to 3 or more times for aggressive behavior, you will be removed from the game and potentially face a season-long ban or worse.

For more on how we play together on the field, see Player Conduct on the Games page.
