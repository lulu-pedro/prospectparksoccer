Games

What happens on the field, and how we play together.

Game Format

The Basics

  • We typically play 7v7, usually 3 games over 2 hours with short breaks in between.
  • We rotate positions between games. There are no subs, everyone always plays.
  • We play rain or shine. (Thunderstorms are different though, we don't mess with lightning.)

Game Day

  • We start each game day with a short speech (important reason to be on time).
  • At the start of each game, beginners and recently injured players raise their hand. Give them extra space, no contact.
  • After teams are divided, players introduce themselves by passing in a circle and sharing their pronouns.
  • After the games, everyone heads to the Adirondack. Come join us. It's half the fun.

Player Conduct

Beginner Friendly

  • Every person should be included in our playing, no matter who they are. Pass to everyone. Give beginners the ball even if they keep missing it.
  • Let people make mistakes. If someone accidentally does a handball to cover their face, don't sweat it. If someone does a throw-in wrong, don't yell at them.
  • If someone says they're injured, respect it immediately, no questions asked.

Mellow Vibes

  • Don't "showboat" and carry the ball, dribble around everyone, score on your own. That's cool other places, not here.
  • Keep your energy mellow, even against equally skilled players.
  • No one should feel that they need to call a foul or end up on the ground. That's a sign the pace is too fast.
  • Be very mindful of physical contact, especially in coed play.
